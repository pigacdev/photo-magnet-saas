# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

**Between:** Magnetoo (Processor) and the Seller (Controller)

**Effective when:** Seller creates a Magnetoo account and accepts the Terms of Service.

## 1. Subject matter

Magnetoo processes personal data on behalf of the Seller when buyers place orders through the Seller's event or storefront links.

## 2. Duration

For the term of the Seller's subscription and until data is deleted per the Privacy Policy and retention schedules.

## 3. Nature and purpose of processing

- Collecting buyer name, email, phone, shipping address, and uploaded photos
- Order fulfilment, seller notifications, buyer confirmation emails
- Image storage, crop processing, and print-ready output generation

## 4. Categories of data subjects

Buyers (end customers of the Seller).

## 5. Categories of personal data

Contact details, order metadata, uploaded photographs.

## 6. Seller obligations (Controller)

- Provide a lawful basis for collecting buyer data
- Respond to buyer data subject requests (Magnetoo provides tools in the dashboard)
- Configure accurate notification emails and pricing
- Do not upload unlawful content

## 7. Magnetoo obligations (Processor)

- Process data only on documented Seller instructions (use of the platform)
- Ensure confidentiality of personnel with access
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (see RoPA)
- Assist with data subject requests where technically feasible
- Notify Seller of personal data breaches without undue delay
- Delete or return data upon termination, subject to legal retention

## 8. Subprocessors

Listed at `/subprocessors` and in `docs/legal/subprocessors.md`. Magnetoo will inform Sellers of material changes.

## 9. International transfers

Where subprocessors transfer data outside the EEA, appropriate safeguards (e.g. SCCs) apply.

## 10. Audit

Seller may request reasonable information about compliance upon written request.
